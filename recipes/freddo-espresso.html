Freddo Espresso

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 shots freshly brewed espresso
  • Sugar,to taste(optional)
  • ice as needed

Direction

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all Ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour espresso and sugar into a cocktail shaker.Add 4 ice cubes,cover, and shake until coffee is frothy and cold and ice has melted

  3. Step 3

    4. Fill the glass with ice and pour shaken coffee over ice
